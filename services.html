Services

For your health:

Radiographs or X-Rays are an important part of your dental visit. It is the most accurate way for us to detect and diagnose potential or existing problems. We utilize digital x-rays for minimal radiation exposure and greater patient comfort.

Amalgam Free

Our office has also made a conscious effort to be amalgam free. Although the use of silver or mercury containing fillings has been widely accepted and used in the past we believe in minimizing risk and the potential exposure of these types of fillings for your overall health and well being.
