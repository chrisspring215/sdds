Meet Our Staff

Travis Sterner DDS

Dr. Sterner is dedicated to providing the community to providing the community with the utmost in comprehensive dental care by utilizing the latest techniques and technology. While continuing education is a high priority for himself, Dr. Sterner also places great importance on educating his patients and the surrounding community. He is well trained in all aspects of general dentistry and continually explores the most recent advances within the field.

Dr. Sterner received his undergraduate degree from Albion College and his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from The University of Michigan School of Dentistry. Dr. Sterner is an active member of the American Dental Association, the Michigan Dental Association, the Washtenaw County District Dental Society, and the Delta Sigma Delta International Dental Fraternity.

When he's not practicing dentistry, Dr. Sterner enjoys a variety of sports and fitness activities: skiing, swimming, tennis, and biking are just a few of his many interests.

Brandi

Brandi is our Registered Dental Hygienist who has a passion for teaching good practices for oral health. Her strengths are in preventative care including periodontal treatment, prophalaxis treatment, and educating the patient to continue to care for there oral health in everyday life. With a degree from the University of Michigan Brandi has gained a wealth of knowledge on dental health such as nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle which can have an overall affect on your oral health as well as your health in general. As a practicing hygienist since 2007 Brandi has successfully treated hundreds of patients from every walk of life.

As a mother of three and a wife Brandi really enjoys working with families. She is often heard swapping stories with other busy moms as well as being able to gain the trust of the children so they look forward to see the dentist. With all her children in sports, scouts, and music programs she understands the logistics and stress of being able to make it to the dentist. However, the time you spend at Sterner Family Dental you can rest assure that your family will be taken well care of.

Sharon L. Libich, RDA, RDH, BS

Sharon has been practicing in dental hygiene since 1979. After being an assistant for six years, she returned to school to become a dental hygienist and 2008 she completed her degree at the University of Detroit Mercy. She is Married to her husband Rick of 34 years with two daughters Michelle and Katie. Michelle is a mechanical engineer graduating from Michigan State University and has a MBA from Indiana University. Katie is a Chemical Engineer graduating from the University of Michigan. Sharon is very fond of spending time with her son in law, Steven Jr. and a one year old Grandson Steven III. In her spare time she loves traveling, sewing, crochet, and caring for patients needs.
