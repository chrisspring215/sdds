Family Dentistry for Whitmore Lake and Washtenaw County

We are a general, cosmetic, and family dental office that is proud to provide high quality dental care in a comfortable environment. We treat each patient as an individual and a friend. Our team of dental professionals works together as a cohesive unit to run an efficient practice, always considerate of our patients time, needs and concerns. We are committed to educating all of our patients on the importance of good dental health, and through creating good dental health we are able to improve overall quality of life. Dr. Sterner and our team will stay knowledgeable on the ever-changing field of dental science with continual learning. Most importantly, we will always strive to provide patients with the very best that dentistry can offer, so that you maintain a healthy, beautiful smile for a lifetime.
